We collect your email address for the purpose of login authentication. Your email address will be stored securely and will not be disclosed to any third parties other than our development team.

We collect your IP address to implement rate limiting and ensure the proper functioning of the Website. Your IP address will be kept confidential and will not be disclosed to any third parties other than our development team.
