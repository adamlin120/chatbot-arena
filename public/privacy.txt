1. Introduction

Welcome to TWLLM Chatbot Arena ("the Website"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Agreement outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure your information remains confidential.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Conversations and Activities

We collect and store all user conversations and activities on the Website. This data is collected for academic and research purposes. Your name, email address, and personal photo will be retained in our database, but they will be anonymized in the data that we disclose to the public. The term "public" refers to anyone except of our development team, National Taiwan University, and the g0v team.  However, your conversation records and other activities will be published on our website as part of an open dataset.

2.2 Email Address

We collect your email address for the purpose of login authentication. Your email address will be stored securely and will not be disclosed to any third parties other than our development team.

2.3 IP Address

We collect your IP address to implement rate limiting and ensure the proper functioning of the Website. Your IP address will be kept confidential and will not be disclosed to any third parties other than our development team.

3. Use of Collected Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

To improve and optimize the performance and functionality of the Website.
To conduct academic and research activities.
To serve as an open dataset.
To ensure the security and integrity of the Website.
To provide user support and communicate with users regarding their account or the Website's services.
To use the information collected as a sponsored project by g0v, a Taiwanese NGO for digital society.

You acknowledge and agree that Chatbot Arena reserves the right to delete, anonymize, or otherwise dispose of any data collected from you without prior notice, provided that such actions do not constitute willful misconduct. In the absence of willful misconduct, Chatbot Arena shall not be liable for any consequences resulting from the deletion, anonymization, or disposal of data.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the personal information we collect and store. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only, including our developers.
